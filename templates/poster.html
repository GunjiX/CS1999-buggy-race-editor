PROJECT POSTER

What is Buggy Editor?

Buggy Editor is a basic website, that will allow you to easily put together an awesome buggy that you can take to the races. With this clear and simple to use web form, making a buggy is easier than ever, we've provided you with all the boxes you need to fill in. Once its done all you have to do is take your buggy to the starting line, and get ready to compete against other people's designs.

IT CAN DO WHAT!!

This editor will allow you to decide values from number of wheels to how many hamster boosters you want, you can even input how you want your flag to look. Once you have chosen and told us what you want the form will calculate the total cost and provide you with a special access code, or "Buggy JSON", that when taken to the race server will become a racing machine to be reckoned with.

Providing you with clear marked out boxes for each part of the buggy will allow you to know exactly what and where you can input the values you want; and to make n easy system easier, there is even a drop down menu for options like Flag Pattern, so you dont need to remeber and type all the options.

When you get to the races you may find that there is a price limit on how much your buggy will cost, but dont worry about doing the calculations, our editor will sort that out for you and show you all the information you need in the "Show Buggies" tab.


So? What are you waiting for? Get out there and win some races!
